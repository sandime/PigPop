Pig Popular: Some Days are Gnarly, Others are Barley

Welcome to Piggington Beach by the Sea, home to Pig Popular and his friends

Pig Popular is Piggington Beach's surfing champion who is challenged by the new pig at school, Mimsy Sage Pig, who is her hometown surfing champ. Mimsy Sage Pig has all the school pigs mesmerized. You could say she is a trendsetter.

Trying to fit in, Pig Popular gets himself a pair of Pigalo boots, the ones that all of the pigs are wearing. Unfortunately, they weren’t made for walking. They weren’t made for skateboarding, either.

"Pig Popular" is a chapter book, suitable for children in grades second to fifth; ages 5 to 10. You can buy it right now on Amazon or iTunes.

Similar books include "Winnie-the-Pooh", Geronimo Stilton and Junie B. Jones.
